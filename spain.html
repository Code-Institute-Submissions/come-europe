Overview

This smoke-free aparthotel features coffee/tea in a common area, dry cleaning and laundry facilities. WiFi in public areas is free. For a bit of entertainment, a flat-screen TV comes with satellite channels, and added amenities include complimentary wireless Internet access and free local calls. Housekeeping is available on request.

Overview

Check in to DoubleTree by Hilton London-Westminster and count on stylish comfort and a signature DoubleTree chocolate chip cookie. Situated in the heart of Westminster and within easy reach of the Houses of Parliament, the London Eye, Tate Britain, London's Theatre District and the Square Mile, our hotel helps you make the most of your time in Britain's capital. Novotel

Previous Next
Overview

Featuring a roof terrace with 360 views across the London skyline, Novotel London Canary Wharf also benefits from an on-site gym, pool and contemporary restaurant and bar. Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel. The Bōkan Restaurant, Bar and Roof Terrace serves artisanal food and drink made using a mixture of local, ancient, and adopted British ingredients. The menu is inspired by European cultural cuisines. There are panoramic views of London and the Thames, high-speed WiFi and triple-story rooftop dining. Greenwich Park is 2.1 miles from Novotel London Canary Wharf, while Tower Bridge is 2.2 miles away. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 3.1 miles from Novotel London Canary Wharf.
