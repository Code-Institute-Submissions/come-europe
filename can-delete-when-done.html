Overview

This smoke-free aparthotel features coffee/tea in a common area, dry cleaning and laundry facilities. WiFi in public areas is free. Other amenities include a 24-hour front desk, a computer station and multilingual staff. Each apartment provides a kitchen with a fridge, a hob, a microwave and a dishwasher. For a bit of entertainment, a flat-screen TV comes with satellite channels, and added amenities include complimentary wireless Internet access and free local calls. Housekeeping is available on request.
